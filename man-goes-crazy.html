Man goes crazy after discovering secrets of the universe

By CTV Staff | August 5, 2025

In an event that some are calling "unprecedented" and others are calling "completely made up," a local man reportedly went completely insane after claiming to have uncovered the secrets of the universe while staring too long at a lava lamp.

Eyewitnesses say the man, who wishes to remain anonymous but is known locally as “That Guy With the Beard,” began shouting mathematical equations in a language no one could understand. He then declared that “everything is triangles” before attempting to levitate using only positive thinking and hummus.

“He kept pointing at clouds and whispering, ‘They’re watching again,’” said one neighbor. “Frankly, it was a little weird even for him.” Authorities arrived on the scene but were unable to determine whether he had actually discovered anything or just eaten too many expired edibles.

Experts remain skeptical. “The secrets of the universe are unlikely to be uncovered during a backyard barbecue,” said Dr. Elaine Morris of the Canadian Rational Society. “But hey, stranger things have happened. I guess.”

The man is currently resting comfortably in a hammock, allegedly communicating with other dimensions through interpretive dance.
